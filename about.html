About this program

This project is the final project for CS534, computational photography in University of Wisconsin-Madison. CS534 official website is here. For the project, all rights are reserved to all 4 group members of CS534 image style transfer group. Our group members have been contributed hugely and equally into this project, and we generated desirable results from our output.

We would like to thank Professor Gupta and all three TA's for their efforts in this class, as they have offered numerous help in our daily work and their dedicating in teaching the materials.
